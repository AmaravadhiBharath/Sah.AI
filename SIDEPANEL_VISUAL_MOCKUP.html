üéØ Key Design Improvements

Visual & Spacing

  • Increased padding from 12px to 16px
  • Better use of whitespace (breathing room)
  • Consistent 8px gap between elements
  • Subtle shadows for depth (0 4px 8px)
  • Smooth border-radius (8px for main, 6px for secondary)
  • Clean color palette (5 neutral + 3 functional)

Interaction & Feedback

  • Hover states show interactive elements
  • Active states scale to 1.02 (subtle feedback)
  • Smooth 200ms transitions (not instant)
  • Focus states clearly visible
  • Empty states feel warm, not punitive
  • Success animations celebrate wins

Content & Hierarchy

  • One primary action per screen
  • Clear platform status (platform badge)
  • Results summary with emojis (visual language)
  • Numbered prompts for scanning
  • Truncated preview text (2 lines max)
  • Meta information (time, word count)

Accessibility & Inclusivity

  • High contrast text (#1a1a1a on white)
  • Min 48x48px touch targets
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Clear focus states
  • Dark theme for eye comfort
  • Large, readable fonts (11-14px)
