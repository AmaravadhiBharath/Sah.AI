## FAQ Section

### Question 1
**How do I use SahAI?**
Install the Chrome extension. Start a conversation on ChatGPT, Claude, or any supported AI platform. When you want to save a prompt, click the SahAI button. It extracts instantly. Done.

### Question 2
**Is my data private?**
Yes. SahAI stores everything locally on your device. We don't access, store, or sync your prompts to any server. Your data never leaves your computer.

### Question 3
**Does it work with all AI platforms?**
SahAI works with ChatGPT, Claude, Google AI, and other major AI chat platforms. We're constantly adding support for new platforms.

### Question 4
**Is SahAI free?**
Yes. All core features are completely free. No hidden charges. No premium upsells.

### Question 5
**Can I export my prompts?**
Yes. Export your entire library as a file anytime. Use it for backup, sharing, or moving to another tool.

### Question 6
**What happens if I uninstall the extension?**
Your saved prompts remain stored in your browser's local data. If you reinstall, they'll be there.
