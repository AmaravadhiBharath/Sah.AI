Extract and summarize prompts from any website

A lightweight Chrome extension that lets you capture prompts with a single click. Fast, private, and built for everyday use.

One click extraction

Click the extension icon on any page and instantly extract prompts. No setup, no friction.

Smart summarization

Automatically condense long prompts into key points. Toggle between full text and summary mode.

Local processing

Everything happens in your browser. Your data never leaves your device. No servers, no tracking.

Clean history

All extracted prompts are saved locally. Search, organize, and revisit any extraction anytime.

Dark mode

Matches your system preferences. Works seamlessly whether you prefer light or dark theme.

Free and open

No subscription fees, no hidden costs. Source code available on GitHub for transparency.

How it works

01

Install the extension

Add Prompt Extractor to your Chrome browser in seconds. No signup required.

02

Visit any website

Go to any page containing prompts or text you want to capture and save.

03

Click to extract

Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar. Choose extract or summarize mode.

04

Use or save

Copy the extracted text, save to history, or export for use in your projects.

Available on Chrome. Free forever.
